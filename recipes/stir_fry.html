Stir Fry

Description

Stir Fry is a classic recipe that is great for clearing leftovers and random veggies from the fridge. It generally takes very little time and effort (and usually only 1 pot) to make this dish and is great for easy meal preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive olive
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 1 cup baby corn
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 garlic clovers
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarcb
  • chopped green onions and sesame seeds for optional garnish

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium-high head in a wok or lage skillet. Add bell pepper, peas, carrots, broccoli, baby corn and water chestnuts. Saute 2-3 munites until veggies are almost tender.
  2. In a small bowl, wisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, veggie broth and cornstarch
  3. Pour over veggies and cook until sauce has thickened. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.
